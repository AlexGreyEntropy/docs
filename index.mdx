Thaler Finance is a Solana protocol that turns a SOL deposit into a delta-neutral yield position. The protocol combines liquid staking, a Kamino borrow loop and a short perpetual hedge so the realised return tracks the rates earned, not the price of SOL.

This documentation explains how the product works at a level useful to depositors, integrators and reviewers. It does not publish strategy parameters, position sizes or operational thresholds.
